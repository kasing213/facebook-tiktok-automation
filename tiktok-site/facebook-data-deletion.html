Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your right to delete your data. Follow the instructions below to request deletion of your data from our application.

Method 1: In-App Deletion

Step 1: Log into your account
Step 2: Go to Settings > Account
Step 3: Click "Disconnect Facebook"
Step 4: Click "Delete All My Data"
Step 5: Confirm deletion

Your data will be permanently deleted within 24 hours.

Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access your account, send an email to:

Email: privacy@yourdomain.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request
Include:
  • Your name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Facebook User ID (if known)

What Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your Facebook access tokens
  • Your profile information
  • All stored Page tokens
  • Your account settings and preferences
  • Any scheduled posts

Timeline

  • In-App Deletion: Immediate (processed within 24 hours)
  • Email Request: We will respond within 5 business days

After Deletion

Once deleted:

  • You will not be able to access your account
  • All data will be permanently removed from our servers
  • This action cannot be undone
  • You can create a new account anytime

Questions?

Contact us at: privacy@yourdomain.com

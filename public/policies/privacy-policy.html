Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. What Data We Collect

Account Information

When you connect your Facebook account, we collect:

  • Your public profile (name, profile picture)
  • Email address
  • List of Facebook Pages you manage
  • Page access tokens (to post on your behalf)

Invoice and Business Data

For our invoice management features, we collect:

  • Customer information (names, contact details)
  • Invoice details (amounts, products, due dates)
  • Payment verification records (verification status, timestamps)
  • Inventory data (product catalogs, stock levels)

Payment Verification Screenshots

Important: When you upload payment screenshots for verification:

  • Screenshots are processed temporarily using OCR (Optical Character Recognition)
  • Images are NOT permanently stored in our database
  • We only retain verification metadata (confidence score, verification status, timestamp)
  • Screenshots may be cached in memory for up to 24 hours for manual review, then automatically deleted
  • All processing is done securely within our encrypted systems

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate you in our application
  • Post content to your Facebook Pages
  • Show you analytics from your Pages
  • Manage automated posting schedules
  • Process invoices and manage customer relationships
  • Verify payments through OCR analysis (temporary processing only)
  • Provide inventory management and stock tracking
  • Send notifications via Telegram when linked

3. Data Security

All access tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

Payment Screenshot Security

For payment verification screenshots:

  • All uploads are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Screenshots are processed in secure, isolated environments
  • No permanent storage of payment images on our servers
  • OCR processing happens in memory and results are immediately returned
  • Any temporary caching is encrypted and automatically purged within 24 hours
  • Access to verification systems is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties. Your data is only used within our application.

Payment Screenshot Data

We NEVER share payment screenshots or extracted payment information with third parties. OCR processing is done entirely within our secure infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

General Data

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, most data is removed within 30 days.

Payment Verification Records

For compliance and audit purposes, we retain verification metadata (non-sensitive information only):

  • Kept: Verification timestamps, confidence scores, approval status, invoice references
  • NOT kept: Actual payment screenshots, account numbers, transaction details
  • Retention period: Up to 7 years for financial audit compliance
  • Access: Limited to authorized personnel for dispute resolution only

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Disconnect your Facebook account anytime
  • Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion)
  • Access your stored data, including verification records
  • Opt out of automated payment verification (manual review only)
  • Request export of your verification history (metadata only)

7. Consent for Payment Verification

By uploading payment screenshots, you explicitly consent to:

  • Temporary processing of your payment screenshot using OCR technology
  • Extraction of payment information for verification purposes
  • Retention of verification metadata (non-sensitive information)
  • Automatic deletion of the screenshot after processing

You can withdraw this consent anytime by disabling payment verification in your account settings.
